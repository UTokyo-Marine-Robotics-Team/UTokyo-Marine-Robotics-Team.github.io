We’ve officially launched the UTokyo Marine Robotics Team.

In this blog, we’ll share development updates, competition reports, and technical learnings in a clear and accessible way.

## What we’ll write about

- Vehicle design (structure, pressure resistance, waterproofing)
- Control and navigation algorithms
- Sensors and underwater communication
- Competition and event reports

Thanks for following along.
