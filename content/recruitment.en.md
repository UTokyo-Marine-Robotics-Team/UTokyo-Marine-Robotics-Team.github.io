# Join Us

The UTokyo Marine Robotics Team is looking for members who want to work together on underwater robot development.

---

## Who We're Looking For

- **Undergraduate and graduate students** enrolled at the University of Tokyo
- Any background welcome: mechanical, electrical, computer science, or related fields
- Interest in the ocean and robotics, and enthusiasm for team-based hands-on projects
